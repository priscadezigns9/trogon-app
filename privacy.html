Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Trogon ("we", "us", or "our") is a social media management platform built by Prisca Dezigns. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at trogon-app.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Facebook data — when you connect a Facebook page, we store your page access token, page name, and page ID to enable posting on your behalf
  • Post content — text, images, and scheduled posts you create in Trogon
  • Usage data — basic analytics about how you use the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To post content to your connected social media pages on your behalf
  • To schedule and manage your posts
  • To show you analytics about your pages
  • To provide customer support

3. Facebook Permissions

When you connect Facebook, we request the following permissions:

  • pages_manage_posts — to publish posts to your pages
  • pages_read_engagement — to read likes and comments
  • pages_show_list — to display your connected pages

We only access data necessary to provide the service. We never post without your explicit instruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is stored securely using Supabase and is only accessible by you and authorised Trogon staff.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@priscadezigns.org or visiting our Data Deletion page.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage to protect your data. Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed publicly.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your Facebook account at any time from Settings
