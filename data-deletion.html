Data Deletion Request

If you connected your Facebook account to Trogon and would like your data removed, you can request deletion at any time. We take your privacy seriously.

1
Email us at hello@priscadezigns.org with the subject line: Data Deletion Request — Trogon
2
Include the email address or Facebook account associated with your Trogon account
3
We will delete all your data within 30 days and send you a confirmation

Data we store includes: your name, email, connected Facebook page tokens, and scheduled post content. We never sell your data to third parties.
